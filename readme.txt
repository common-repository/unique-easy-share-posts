== Description ==

#### Unique Easy Share Posts

The complete option for admin to share Google Shorten URL posts by selecting the post from dropdown with display of featured image and enable the social share links to share the post.
**Unique Easy Share Posts - Features:**

*   Dropdown with all the posts - Unique Easy Share Posts
*   Select a post from dropdown to share  - Unique Easy Share Posts
*   Automatic display featured image by selecting the post without refresh - Unique Easy Share Posts
*   Share the selected post with one click - Unique Easy Share Posts
*	Share more than one post on the same page without refreshing the page - Unique Easy Share Posts
*	Social share buttons included Facebook, Twitter, Google+, LinkedIn, Pinterest, Tumblr, Digg, Reddit, StumbleUpon and share through Email Button - Unique Easy Share Posts
*	Add More Social Share Links Button to add any other social media link - Unique Easy Share Posts

Unique Easy Share Posts team constantly works on upgrades and improvements. With our upcoming updates we are planning to add more social media share links.

Install Unique Easy Share Posts either via the WordPress.org plugin directory, or by uploading the files to your server via FTP.

Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

Go to the Unique Easy Share Posts settings and select the post you want to share.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

**How can I enable the social share links?**

You can enable the share links by selecting the post from dropdown.

**Can I have share more than one post on the same page?**

You can share more than one post as you want on the same page, but you can only set one popup to be opened onpage load, the others must be set to open on wait time.
